Rainbow Veggie Salad

15 mins Serves 2–3 Light • Fresh

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 small carrot, shredded
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup feta (optional)
  • Handful of nuts or seeds
  • Salt & pepper

Directions

  1. Add all salad ingredients to a large bowl.
  2. In a small jar, shake 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1 tsp honey, a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Pour dressing over salad, toss gently, and serve immediately.

Make it yours

  • Add grilled chicken or chickpeas for protein.
  • Swap lemon for balsamic vinegar.
  • Top with avocado for extra creaminess.
