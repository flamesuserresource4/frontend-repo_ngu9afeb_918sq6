Berry Blast Smoothie

5 mins Serves 1–2 Breakfast • Snack

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (or dairy‑free)
  • 1/2 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Ice cubes, if needed
  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, 30–60 seconds. Adjust liquid for desired thickness.
  3. Taste and sweeten more if needed. Pour into a glass and enjoy.

Variations

  • Swap berries for mango + pineapple.
  • Add spinach for a green boost.
  • Top with granola and serve as a smoothie bowl.
